Welcome to the central repository of all the EarlyON playgroups in Ottawa! Finally, a place where parents can find details about all the EarlyON playgroups in the city. EarlyON offers weekday and weekend playgroups. There are even some evening playgroups listed!

Are you looking for free activities for babies, toddlers or young children? Explore our comprehensive list of EarlyON playgroups near you!

Do you have feedback? Are you interested in volunteering to help keep this website up to date? Did you notice an error in the playgroups listed? I'd be happy to hear from you! This tool was built by Claudie Larouche with lots of help from ChatGPT, feel free to connect on Facebook or LinkedIn!

Now that I have some good data to play with, I've started making a few charts. :)

lightbulb Please make sure to always click on the Playgroup Name link to verify that the playgroup you are planning to visit is still running. Please note that I am not always informed of last minute cancellations.
