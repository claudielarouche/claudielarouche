Hello!

I am writing these words on April 13, 2024. A few days ago, I launched a website that showed all the pool time in the City of Ottawa

Originally I copied the schedule by hand, one pool at a time.

But that was taking forever and I got frustrated wasting so much of my time. So instead, I asked my friend ChatGPT to write a script to extract the pool schedule from all the City of Ottawa facilities

After a few hours, I realized that not only was I able to extract all the swimming schedule, I was also able to extract the schedule of every single activity posted online on Ottawa facility pages

So since I have this information, I might as well give it away :)

I'm not sure if this will become a maintenance nightmare or not, I don't know how often the schedule changes... If you notice that some data is outdated, please send me a note at claudielarouche@gmail.com and I'll update the entire schedule (It shouldn't be too too long to do)

If you could please share this website with all your friends and family, I would be super grateful!

If you know anyone from the media or someone who works at the City of Ottawa, I would also appreciate if you mention this website to them. I'd love to get the word out as much as possible, and I would be very happy to share my code with the city if they'd like to take this on

Make sure you check out my other projects too, I published a few other cool stuff

Thanks for visiting this website, enjoy!
