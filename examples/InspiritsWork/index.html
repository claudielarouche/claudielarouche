Many now recognize that at the root of our present crises — individually and collectively — is a separation from Spirit, Essence, the Divine. Healing this separation is possible when we awaken to who we really are — body, heart, mind, soul and spirit; when we honour our ancestors; when we access and express the power of our creative spirits; and when we say “Yes!” to Life.
 

Woman Spirit Rising, by Diana Claire Douglas 
 
Explore the infinite possibilities for integrating your life with Spirit and expressing your soul in your daily life. Diana Claire Douglas offers a variety of services as your companion and guide through your awakening process.
