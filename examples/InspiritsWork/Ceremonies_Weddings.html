Officiating for Ceremonies and Weddings

 

As a non-denominational minister, ordained through CoreLight (in the US) and All Season’s Church of Canada, Diana is delighted to offer officiating services for weddings — civil, spiritual, and personal — and other ceremonies.

Wedding Ceremonies
Love is weaving the tapestry of your lives. Love is calling you to be together.
You are ready to make a commitment to your loved one, surrounded by family and friends.
You wish to create a ceremony that is truly a reflection of who you both are.
You wish to have your small or large wedding in a special setting — for instance, in a restaurant, park, or your home.
 
As your wedding celebrant, Diana offers you:
~ guidance in creating your ceremony exactly as you wish it to be. Sample ceremonies are available
~ a calm and loving presence
~ her experience gained from years of facilitating group events
~ an honouring of all personal and spiritual paths

“There was so much love in the air on our wedding day and we feel our beautiful ceremony spun a lot of that magic.”

 

Diana also is a celebrant for other special ceremonies and celebrations:
~ naming or baptism ceremonies
~ rites of passage
~ memorial services


Please contact All Season’s Church of Canada to request Diana’s services as a celebrant or officiant. Call 613-821-1106 or 800-545-3681
