Values

Respect

As a young boy growing up my parents taught me at an early age that respect would play an important role in my journey through life. Respecting others regardless of race, religion, sexual orientation or gender is fundamental in growing up. Appreciating the diversity of people and their culture will make positive changes in my life and develop long-term friendship and relation with others.

Determination

Throughout my life I have always been a determined person in achieving my goals even though at times there maybe obstacles in the way. Being a determined person has taught me to never give up and stick to my goals in life.

Commitment

Commitment is all about being true to yourself, being accountable for your actions, and even more delivering on your commitment. Integrity of my word and follow-through is important to me as it will make me a better person and gain the trust of people that I meet in my life.
