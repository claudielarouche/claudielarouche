The smaller the gym the better the workout

If you want to get in serious shape, repetitive movements can only restrict you; Machines will limit your body's own abilities and create unbalances that can lead to injury. If you prefer an athletic look, you have to train like an athlete.

Many people believe that training from home may not be challenging enough, or may not bring results but in fact the opposite is true: Programs designed without heavy gym equipment are not only sound and time-efficient, they bring true results which translates into a functional, lean and toned body. The key is to use your body in its own structural integrity.

Functional movement training

The most challenging and effective workouts are achieved with your own body-weight or using simple yet efficient tools such as a stability ball and exercises bands, allowing your body to develop and strengthen in a natural and balanced way.

Core Training

Your core is the foundation of your body, this is why your limbs can only be as strong as your core. A weak core leads to decreased performance, bad posture, back pain and injury, lack of stability as well as the inability to use your body as a whole, a decreased metabolism and a muffin-top shape.
